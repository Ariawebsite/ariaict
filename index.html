Introduction

Welcome to the Journal of Business and Social Science, your premier destination for cutting-edge research and scholarly discourse. Established to promote excellence in academic and practical insights, our journal is committed to fostering a vibrant platform where innovative ideas converge. By bridging the realms of academia and industry, we aspire to make meaningful contributions to the fields of business and social science.

As an inclusive journal, we take pride in collaborating with a global community of researchers, practitioners, and thought leaders. We recognize the importance of diverse voices and perspectives in addressing the complex challenges of our time. By facilitating cross-disciplinary and cross-cultural exchanges, we aim to broaden the horizons of knowledge and foster innovation that transcends borders.

We invite you to explore our archives, browse through the latest articles, and contribute to our growing body of knowledge. Whether you are a researcher seeking a platform to share your findings, a student aspiring to learn from the best minds, or a practitioner looking for actionable insights, the Journal of Business and Social Science has something for you.

Together, let us shape the future of business and society through research, dialogue, and collaboration. Welcome aboard, and we look forward to embarking on this intellectual journey with you.
