About Our Journals

Explore Our Research

The Journal of Business and Social Science offers a platform for innovative research in various fields, including business strategies, social policies, and global economic trends. Each issue provides valuable insights to scholars, practitioners, and students.

Our mission is to empower researchers and practitioners with cutting-edge findings that drive meaningful change. We publish articles that span business strategies, global economic trends, social innovation, and interdisciplinary collaborations.

Each issue of the journal undergoes a rigorous peer-review process, ensuring that every publication meets the highest standards of academic integrity and impact. Our readership includes academics, policymakers, and industry leaders who rely on our journal for credible and actionable insights.

By bridging the gap between theory and practice, the Journal of Business and Social Science aims to inspire transformative ideas and foster a global community of change-makers.
